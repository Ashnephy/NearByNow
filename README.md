# 🌊 NearByNow

**Find cities within X minutes of any US city.**

[![GitHub Pages](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub-Pages-blue)](https://pages.github.com/)
[![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-green)](LICENSE)

## ✨ Features

- 🔍 **Radius Search**: Find cities within 5-60 minutes of any US city
- 📏 **Distance Calculator**: Get driving time and miles between two cities
- 🌙 **Dark Mode**: Toggle between ocean light and neon dark themes
- 🔥 **Streak Tracking**: Track your daily city discoveries
- 📜 **Search History**: Quick access to recent searches
- ⚡ **CSS Animations**: Smooth, modern interactions
- 📱 **Responsive**: Works on all devices
